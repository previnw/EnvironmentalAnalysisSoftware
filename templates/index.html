Environmental Analysis

Problem

Environmental factors that can affect human life, such as harmful emissions and air quality control, are difficult to monitor unless physically monitored.

Goal

Provide a platform to monitor air and environmental factors remotely in order to prevent harm to human life and at risk resources such as perishable organic substances.

Action

Build an Embedded System to record data from target areas and display on external webpage.
